ENERGIZING EDUCATION PROGRAMME (EEP)

A Federal Government of Nigeria initiative providing sustainable, clean power to 37 federal universities and 7 university teaching hospitals nationwide.

About EEP

Access to reliable electricity in Nigeriaâ€™s educational and healthcare institutions has long been a critical challenge, one that directly undermines the quality of teaching, research, student residency, and the delivery of essential medical services. This persistent energy deficit has remained a significant barrier to institutional performance and national development. Recognising the foundational role of education and health in driving socio-economic progress, the Federal Government of Nigeria, through the Federal Ministry of Power and the Rural Electrification Agency (REA), launched the Energizing Education Programme (EEP), a strategic intervention designed to transform power access across public tertiary institutions in the country.
