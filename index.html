ENERGIZING EDUCATION PROGRAMME (EEP)

A Federal Government of Nigeria initiative providing sustainable, clean power to 37 federal universities and 7 university teaching hospitals nationwide.

About EEP

The Energizing Education Programme (EEP) is an initiative of the Federal Government of Nigeria (FGN), implemented by the Rural Electrification Agency, through the Nigeria Electrification Programme (NEP), tasked with providing clean, reliable and sustainable power to 37 Federal Universities and 7 affiliated University Teaching Hospitals across Nigeria.
The project plan includes the provision of solar hybrid power plants, upgrading of existing distribution infrastructure, installation of street lighting to improve security within the Universities' campuses and the construction of world-class renewable energy training centres for each university.
