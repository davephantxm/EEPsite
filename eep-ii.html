EEP Phase II

EEP Phase II expands the reach of sustainable, clean energy to more universities and teaching hospitals across Nigeria, fostering innovation, research, and growth. This phase builds on the success of Phase I, delivering reliable power and supporting the next generation of leaders and innovators.

Universities Benefitting from Phase II

  • University of Lagos (UNILAG)
  • Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU)
  • University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN)
  • Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB)
  • University of Calabar (UNICAL)
  • University of Maiduguri (UNIMAID)
  • Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO)
  • Ahmadu Bello University Teaching Hospital (ABUTH)
