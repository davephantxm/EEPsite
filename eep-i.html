OVERALL PROGRESS & NEXT STEPS

TECHNICAL


CURRENT STATUS

  1. Preliminary and detailed design drawings are undergoing reviews while over 90% have been approved.
  2. Civil construction has been completed in all Solar sites while civil works have commenced at the gas sites.
  3. Fencing works have been completed across all the 9 project sites.
  4. Equipment foundation works have reached completion in 8 out of 9 sites
  5. FATs on major equipment have been conducted for all the project sites.
  6. Access and Internal Roads and Site Drainages have been completed at most of the Solar sites, while same is ongoing for some other sites.
  7. All major equipment has been installed at all the Solar sites, while major equipment for the Gas sites have been purchased and ready for shipment/delivery to the project sites, however this will be carried out upon the required payment that is due to the contractors for these sites.
  8. Installation of Streetlights have commenced across all sites and have been completed across 5 project sites.
  9. The FUNAI and BUK project sites were commissioned on 2nd August and 3rd September 2019 respectively, while ATBU and FUPRE are the next project sites to be commissioned.
  10. Workshop/training Center for FUAM, NAU and UDUS are in progress, while UNILAG and OAU are at various stages of construction. The WTC materials for the Gas sites are however on site and construction of the WTC will commence as soon as practicable, considering the COVID-19 situation.
  11. In addition to the commissioned sites, PV Panel installation has also been completed across all the solar sites.
  12. Pre-commissioning Tests have been completed at FUPRE, ATBU and FUAM

FOCUS ITEMS FOR NEXT REPORTING PERIOD

  1. Continue ongoing review and approval of the detailed design drawings.
  2. Continue ongoing civil construction works on gas sites.
  3. Hold Progress Meetings with Contractors.
  4. Continue ongoing streetlights installations and construction of the Workshop and Training Centers.
  5. Complete PV Panel alignment at the FUAM site.
  6. Develop Sustainability Plan Governance Structure/Model

FINANCIAL


CURRENT STATUS

  1. All project sites have received complete mobilization payment from the Sovereign Green Bond Fund and FGN.
  2. Except for UNILAG which is yet to receive 3rd payment, all other project sites have received full 2nd and 3rd payment from the Sovereign Green Bond Fund & FGN.
  3. In addition, ATBU and OAU sites have received up to 4th payment, FUAM has received up to 6th payment, while FUNAI and BUK have received complete payment
  4. Invoices for further payments have been received for some sites, however this has not been paid due to unavailability of funds.

FOCUS ITEMS FOR NEXT REPORTING PERIOD

  1. Payment on all outstanding milestone invoices.
  2. Funding from Development partners and donor organizations, in order to fund the EEP Phase I Project Management Team.
  3. Renewal of contracts for members of the Review Team and Site/Resident Engineers
  4. Source for special intervention funds to pay the EPC Contractors for additional costs incurred on the EEP Phase 1 projects.
