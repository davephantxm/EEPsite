Science,

Mathematics (STEM)

The EEP STEM Programme is designed to provide female students in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics with hands-on practical training and experience in the design and construction of power systems. This initiative aims to bridge the gender gap in STEM fields and empower young women to become leaders in Nigeria's energy sector.

Participants gain real-world skills by working alongside industry professionals during the construction phase of EEP projects, fostering innovation, confidence, and career opportunities in sustainable energy.

Programme Impact

Inspiring and equipping the next generation of female STEM leaders to drive Nigeria's sustainable future.

Hands-on Experience

Participants work directly on real EEP projects, gaining practical skills in renewable energy systems.

Mentorship

Guidance from industry experts and role models to foster growth, confidence, and career development.

Gender Inclusion

Promoting gender diversity in STEM and empowering women to lead in Nigeria's energy sector.
